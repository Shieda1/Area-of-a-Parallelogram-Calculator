What is the area of a parallelogram calculator?
The area of a parallelogram is the space that is enclosed with in it's four sides. The area of a parallelogram is equal to the product of length and height of the parallelogram.
How to calculate area of a parallelogram calculator?
To calculate area of a parallelogram calculator you should use the following formula:
A = b * h
where:
b = base
h = height
